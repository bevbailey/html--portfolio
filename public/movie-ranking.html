Best movies according to Bev

Top 4 movies of 2023

Talk To Me

This movie was not only a wild ride but scary as well. The ending will leave you thinking about the movie for weeks. 10/10.

Wakanda Forever

Emotional and inspiring. Hate to love characters, scifi and world building were great. RIP Chad.

The Harder They Fall

Based on real life people. Wild Wild West with a little comedy. Great story.

Creed 3

Michael B. Jordan is good at anything he acts in honestly. This story follows Adonis and his family as he deals with a different set of problems that comes with fame and having a family. Highly recommend this movie.
